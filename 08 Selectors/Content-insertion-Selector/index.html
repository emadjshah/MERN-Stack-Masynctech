Fluffy Pancakes

Prep time:15 mins Cook time:20 mins

    Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Milk

Instructions

1. Start by mixing all the dry ingredients in a bowl.

2. Whish in the eggs and milk until you gave a smooth batter.

3. Cook each side on a medium heat until golden brown.

Tip: Serve with fresh berries and maple syrup for added flavor!
